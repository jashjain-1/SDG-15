What is the Miyawaki Method?

The Miyawaki Technique, developed by Dr. Akira Miyawaki, is an innovative afforestation approach. It enables forest growth up to 10 times faster, creates 30 times higher density, and supports 100 times more biodiversity than conventional plantation methods.

How It Works

This method involves selecting native tree species, enriching the soil with organic materials, planting saplings very close together, and allowing natural succession to accelerate growth. Within 23 years, the forest becomes fully self-sustaining, requiring minimal or no human intervention.
